Our Services

01
Content Creation
We develop custom-written academic content across subjects and educational levels. Our writers blend domain expertise with curriculum alignment to create engaging and accurate material. Whether for textbooks, e-learning platforms, or assessments, our content ensures clarity, consistency, and instructional value.
02
Alt Text Authoring & Review
Our team specializes in creating meaningful, WCAG-compliant alternative text for educational visuals—STEM diagrams, graphs, mathematical expressions, and chemical equations. We ensure that each alt text accurately conveys essential information for screen readers, enhancing accessibility. Review services are also offered to validate existing alt text for compliance and quality.
03
Image Description
We provide detailed and pedagogically sound descriptions for complex visuals, especially in STEM content. Our image descriptions go beyond basic labeling to offer clear, contextually relevant explanations that support visually impaired learners and improve overall content comprehension.
04
Copy Editing & Proofreading
Our copy editors refine content by improving grammar, punctuation, sentence structure, clarity, and tone. We ensure alignment with academic style guides such as APA, MLA, AMA, and Chicago. Our services cover books, journal manuscripts, reports, and learning materials.
05
Quality Checking
We follow a multi-layered quality control process to ensure error-free outputs. This includes proofreading, consistency checks, reference validation, formatting review, and adherence to client-specific guidelines. Each deliverable undergoes thorough scrutiny before submission.
06
Image-to-Text Conversion
We convert printed, handwritten, or scanned visual content into editable digital text. This includes diagrams, math problems, annotations, and complex layout elements. Our service ensures accurate transcription while maintaining formatting and content structure.
07
Normal Text ↔ Equation Conversion
We provide accurate conversion of normal text into mathematical, physical, and chemical equations, and vice versa, with proper formatting, symbols, and notation. Superscripts, subscripts, operators, and scientific symbols are carefully handled to deliver error-free, publication-ready content.
08
Tooltip & UI/UX Text Design
We create clear, concise, and accessible button texts and tooltips that guide users seamlessly through digital platforms. By combining action-oriented language with intuitive design, we ensure interactive elements like buttons, labels, and tips enhance usability, accessibility, and overall user experience.
09
Q&A Generation, Mock Tests & Assessment Design
We develop well-structured questions that are carefully framed, appropriately graded, and supported with accurate answer keys and clear explanations. Subject-wise mock tests are designed for competitive exams, academic assessments, and internal evaluations, with balanced difficulty levels and analytics-ready formatting. Our solutions are ideal for schools, edtech platforms, and publishers.
10
Workflow Automation (Excel-Based Tools)
We design customized Excel automation tools using VBA and macros to streamline publishing workflows. These include auto-collation of data, error flagging, alt text template generation, bulk classification, automated quality checks, and reporting. Our solutions minimize manual effort, reduce errors, and ensure faster, scalable, and high-quality project delivery for publishers, edtech platforms, and content teams.

📦 We offer end-to-end solutions, from content ideation to delivery, backed by a team of subject matter experts, writers, and editors.

We blend human expertise with technology to provide scalable, high-quality solutions.
