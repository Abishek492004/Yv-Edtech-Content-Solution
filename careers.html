Careers

🧑‍💻Join Our Remote Team

At Yv EdTech Content Solutions, we're always on the lookout for skilled freelancers, dedicated editors, and aspiring interns who are passionate about transforming academic content. We offer flexible, remote opportunities designed for students, educators, and professionals across India.

🌍An Inclusive & Collaborative Workplace

We are proud to be an equal opportunity organization that celebrates diversity in all its forms. Whether you're a fresher, a career re-starter, or a subject expert—we welcome applicants from all educational backgrounds, regions, and walks of life.

  • ✅Join a team that values skill, commitment, and collaboration over titles and degrees.

Our Selection Process

Yv EdTech Content Solutions follows a three-round hiring process: an online assessment with a 75-point minimum, onboarding and training with sample work, then selection and verification only after the sample meets our quality standards. Click Apply Now to begin the assessment.

✅ Who We're Looking For

If you have a keen eye for detail, strong grammar and language skills, and the ability to learn quickly, then this is the right place for you. We welcome:

    ✅Interns & Freshers – Kickstart your career with training, mentorship, and real-world projects.

    ✅Freelancers – Work flexibly on diverse projects with clear guidelines and timely payments.

    ✅Part-Time & Full-Time Roles – Build your career in content creation, editing, accessibility solutions, and more.

🚀Join us and grow with a team that values your talent!

  • ✅Whether you're a student, educator, or experienced editor, we have a role for you.
  • 🚀Our team is 100% remote, deadline-driven, and collaborative.

📌Note

Shortlisted applicants will be contacted via email. All projects and positions are subject to availability and assignment.

🚫Who Should Not Apply

If you are an experienced professional looking to shift your career into accessibility, we welcome your application. However, this role may not be suitable for professionals with more than 5 years of experience, as the pay structure may not align with your career expectations.

We value AI as a supportive tool, but it cannot replace human expertise. If you rely entirely on AI without the ability to read, understand, and complete your work, this is not the right place for you. We are looking for professionals who can think critically, analyze, and apply knowledge with accuracy. Blind AI copy-paste workers, please do not apply.

If you are not willing to spend time learning, practicing on samples, and completing tasks within the required turnaround time (TAT), this opportunity is not for you. We expect candidates to be punctual, consistent, and serious about their work.

We believe in commitment, accountability, and continuous learning. Only apply if you are ready to grow with us by contributing real effort and quality work.
